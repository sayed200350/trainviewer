Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BahnBlitz beta

When will the beta start?

The BahnBlitz beta is currently active! We're accepting new testers and rolling out TestFlight invites within 24 hours of approval.

Is the beta free?

Yes! Beta access is completely free. You'll only need a valid TestFlight invitation to download and test the app.

What devices are supported?

BahnBlitz supports iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch running iOS 16.0 or later. Voice commands will require iOS 16+ when available.

Will my data be safe?

Absolutely. We follow strict privacy guidelines and never share your personal information. All beta feedback is anonymous unless you explicitly choose to share it.

How do I provide feedback?

Once you're in the beta, you can submit feedback directly through TestFlight or contact us through the app. Your input helps shape the final product!

When will voice commands be available?

Voice commands are currently in active development! We're working hard to implement Siri integration. Beta testers will be the first to get access when it's ready. Join now to help us test and shape this feature!

When will the app be released?

We're targeting a Q2 2025 App Store release. Beta testing will continue through Q1 2025 to ensure everything works perfectly for German commuters.
